To configure the search please do the following:
  1. Head to your preferred site (e.g. amazon.com) in another tab.
  2. Use the sites search feature to search for the following: SEARCH
  3. Copy the full URL and paste it into the "Configure site" field below. The URL should look something like this:
    "https://www.amazon.com/s?k=SEARCH&browser=chrome&ref=nav_bar".
  4. The fields "Search site" and "Search parameter" should then be filled out automatically! Click on "Save" below to save your changes.
